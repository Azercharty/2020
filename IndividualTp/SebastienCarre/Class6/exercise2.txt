How to setup a camera on the turtlebot :

	First, you have to plug the camera to the raspberry.
	Then, setting up the camera hardware to enable the camera interface.
	And reboot the Raspberry.

What to do to make ROS working on 2 computers :

	We need to launch ssh and run the master.
	Then, we start a listener by configuring ROS_MASTER_URI.
	Finally, we start a talker on the second computer.
